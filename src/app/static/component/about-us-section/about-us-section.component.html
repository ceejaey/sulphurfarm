About Us

"At Sulphur Farm, we believe in nurturing both the mythical beauty of our fire horses and the well-being of the humans who cherish them."

CEO, Alexander "Alex" Thornton

Welcome to Sulphur Farm, where myth and reality converge to bring you the majestic beauty of fire horses. Nestled in the heart of a serene and mystical landscape, Sulphur Farm is dedicated to breeding and raising these extraordinary creatures, whose fiery spirit and ethereal grace captivate the imagination and inspire awe.

At Sulphur Farm, we pride ourselves on our commitment to the care and well-being of our fire horses. Our team of expert breeders, trainers, and caretakers work tirelessly to ensure that each horse is nurtured with the utmost love and respect. Our state-of-the-art facilities provide a safe and comfortable environment, allowing our horses to thrive and develop their unique abilities.
